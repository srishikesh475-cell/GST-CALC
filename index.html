Tax Components

CGST & SGST (Intra-state)

Applied to transactions within the same state. The total GST rate is split equally: half goes to the Central Government (CGST) and half to the State Government (SGST).

IGST (Inter-state)

Applied to transactions between different states. The full GST rate is collected by the Central Government and then shared with the destination state.

currency_exchange

Input Tax Credit (ITC)

ITC is a fundamental mechanism of GST designed to eliminate the cascading effect of taxes ("tax on tax"). It allows businesses to claim credit for taxes paid on purchases and use it to offset the taxes payable on final sales.

Essentially, you only pay tax on the value added at your stage of the supply chain, serving as a vital anti-inflationary tool.
